Why Amplify

Your agent needs
project context

Note-taking apps send individual files to AI with zero context about your voice, your audience, or your workflow. Amplify gives your agent a full project — CLAUDE.md instructions, voice profiles, and writing conventions — every time.

📋

CLAUDE.md at the root

Like .eslintrc for your writing. Your voice rules, anti-patterns, and commands live in a project config that the agent reads on every interaction.

🧬

Voice DNA

One command analyzes your past writing and builds a voice profile. Every draft, edit, and brainstorm runs through it automatically.

📁

Isolated workspaces

Each writing project is its own folder with its own context. Your newsletter and your technical blog don't bleed into each other.

⌨️

Built-in terminal

Claude Code runs right inside the app. Chain commands, iterate on drafts, see exactly what the agent does. No chat sidebar.

📄

Plain Markdown, plain folders

No proprietary format, no database, no sync layer. Your files are just files. Git-native. Open them in anything.

🔑

Bring your own subscription

Uses Claude Code, which you already have. No API key, no extra billing, no account. Free and open source.
